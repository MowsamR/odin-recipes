Veggie Chinese Curry Recipe

Recipe

DESCRIPTION

Chinese Veggie Curry Recipe: Indulge in the rich and aromatic flavors of this Chinese-inspired vegetable curry. Packed with a colorful assortment of fresh vegetables and a blend of fragrant spices, this dish offers a delightful balance of textures and a savory taste that will leave you craving more.

INGREDIENTS

For the tofu:

  • 400 g firm tofu - pressed, for at least a few hours
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

For the rest of the curry:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 onions - cut into big chunks
  • 1 green chilli - finely chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic - finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger powder
  • ¼ teaspoon cumin powder
  • ¼ teaspoon Chinese five spice
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 500 g chestnut mushrooms - quartered
  • 800 ml vegetable stock
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour mixed with a little cold water to make a paste
  • 1 cup peas or petit pois
  • 200 g broccoli - cut into florets

DIRECTIONS

  1. Press the tofu for a few hours (optional), then chop into chunks. [400 g firm tofu]
  2. Coat the tofu in the cornflour on all sides. [2 tablespoons cornflour]
  3. Fry the tofu in 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil until brown on all sides.
  4. Once done, set the tofu aside.
  5. Heat the rest of the oil in a wok and then add the onions. Cook for a few minutes until they start to soften. [2 onions, 1 tablespoon vegetable oil]
  6. Add the chilli and garlic, cook for a couple of minutes more. [6 cloves garlic, 1 green chilli]
  7. Next, add all the spices and cook for a minute until they become fragrant. [2 tablespoons curry powder, 1 teaspoon ginger powder, ¼ teaspoon cumin powder, ¼ teaspoon Chinese five spice, 1 teaspoon turmeric powder]
  8. Pop the mushrooms in the wok and cook for 5 minutes. [500 g chestnut mushrooms]
  9. Add the vegetable stock, then the cornflour mix and stir well. [800 ml vegetable stock, 2 tablespoons cornflour]
  10. Keep it over medium heat, and it will start to thicken. Continue to stir. Add a splash more water if it looks too thick.
  11. Finish the dish by adding the broccoli, tofu, and peas, and cook for another 5-6 minutes. [1 cup peas, 200 g broccoli, 400 g firm tofu]
  12. Serve with rice and enjoy.
